## Case Studies

### Logistics — Automated Freight Operations

**The Problem**
A mid-size freight brokerage was scaling fast, but manual coordination across dispatch, tracking, and customer communications created bottlenecks at every stage. Dispatchers were spending hours on repetitive tasks instead of managing exceptions and building carrier relationships. Missed status updates frustrated customers, and the company was hiring to keep up with volume rather than improving throughput per person.

**What Was Built**
An intelligent automation layer across the core freight workflow. The system handles order intake — parsing incoming requests, matching them to available carriers, and generating dispatch instructions automatically. Status tracking was automated end-to-end with real-time updates pulled from carrier systems, proactive exception flags, and customer notifications sent without dispatcher intervention. An exception-handling module triages disruptions by severity, suggests resolution paths, and escalates only the cases that truly need human judgment.

**Key Results**
- **3+ hours** of capacity recovered per dispatcher per day
- **0** manual status updates required
- **4 weeks** from kickoff to deployment

**Outcome**
Dispatchers recovered hours of productive capacity daily, immediately redirected to carrier negotiations and high-value client relationships. Customer satisfaction improved as status updates became real-time and proactive. Headcount growth was decoupled from shipment volume, fundamentally improving the unit economics of the business.

### Hospitality — Full Guest Operations Platform

**The Problem**
A multi-property hospitality group managing vacation rentals across Dubai had outgrown their tools. Guest communication arrived across WhatsApp, Airbnb, and Booking.com — each handled separately. Reservation data lived in one system, property details in spreadsheets, escalations in group chats, and sales tracking nowhere at all. Staff were the glue holding everything together, and as the portfolio grew, the team was hiring linearly to keep up.

**What Was Built**
A complete guest operations platform from the ground up managing the full guest lifecycle — from reservation sync to post-departure feedback — across every property from a single dashboard. An AI concierge handles guest communication 24/7 across WhatsApp, Airbnb, and Booking.com, drawing from per-property knowledge bases. A multi-level escalation system routes issues to supervisors, managers, or owners with configurable timeouts. Automated message scheduling manages the entire guest journey: pre-arrival information, arrival-day access instructions, mid-stay recommendations, and post-departure feedback collection. The platform integrates with existing property management and booking systems, keeping a single source of truth without manual data entry.

**Key Results**
- **80%** of guest communication handled automatically
- **3 channels** unified into one platform
- **24/7** AI concierge availability

**Outcome**
The platform replaced a patchwork of spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and disconnected tools with a single system running across every property. The group can now add properties without proportionally adding operations staff. Escalations are tracked and timed instead of lost in group chats. Sales performance is visible and measurable.

### Industrial Supply Chain — Unified Communication Layer

**The Problem**
An industrial supplier managing hundreds of SKUs across dozens of suppliers and customers had a critical communication breakdown. Orders came in via WhatsApp, confirmations went out by email, and status updates happened over phone calls — nothing centralized. The operations team spent most of their day chasing information across channels, with frequent errors, slow response times, and increasingly impatient customers.

**What Was Built**
A unified communication layer combining WhatsApp automation and a Voice AI agent. Customers and suppliers interact through their preferred channels — WhatsApp, phone, or email — and every interaction feeds into a single system of record. The WhatsApp automation handles order placement, confirmation, and status enquiries. The Voice AI agent manages phone-based interactions, capturing order details, providing delivery updates, and routing complex requests to the right team member. All communications are logged, structured, and searchable.

**Key Results**
- **1** unified source of truth for all orders
- **3 channels** consolidated into one system
- **60%+** reduction in manual data entry

**Outcome**
The company now has a single source of truth for every order and status update across the supply chain. Response times improved dramatically — customers get instant confirmations instead of waiting for a callback. The operations team shifted from chasing information to managing exceptions. Order errors dropped as manual data entry was replaced by structured, automated capture.
